I Speak – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-01-01

What we collect

  • Account and profile data (email, display name)
  • Learning data (progress, achievements, notes)
  • Device permissions you grant (microphone for speaking practice; camera/photo library for profile images)
  • Notifications you configure (local reminders)
  • Analytics (optional; can be turned off in-app)

How we use data

To provide learning features, sync your progress, improve the app (if analytics enabled), and send local reminders you opt into.

Third parties

We use Supabase for auth, database, and storage. YouTube content may be embedded for listening exercises.

Account deletion

You can delete your account and data anytime in the app: Profile → Settings → Privacy & Data → Delete My Account.
